Maximum Reality — Epilogue

The story of Nora and the threads of her existence has reached its ultimate convergence. Every fear faced, every fragment lost, every blackened strand and radiant pulse has woven together into a single, unbreakable weave. She is the Loom. She is the threads. She is whole.

Through the forests, the alleys of Baghdad, the crumbling caves, the cosmic battlefields, and the hospital rooms of time, she has learned the greatest truth:

The strength of the weave is not in avoiding brokenness—but in embracing it.

And now, the journey is complete. Yet in every ending, new threads shimmer, hinting at worlds waiting to be touched, lives waiting to be healed, realities waiting to be woven.
